About Us

The story began over a decade ago when a group of ambitious entrepreneurs, passionate about providing safe and fast transportation services that meet the local market needs, came together. The company’s founders committed to the highest safety and quality standards, and sought to apply the latest technologies, turning the idea into the reality known as "Saud Saad Alharbi Transport Company". Today, we continue to grow confidently and strive to be the first choice in transportation within the Kingdom.

Our Services

Petroleum Transport Service

At Saud Saad Alharbi Transport Company, we offer petroleum transport service with high efficiency and professionalism. We adhere to the highest safety and quality standards on every trip. We rely on a modern fleet and trained team to ensure safe and fast delivery. Our goal is to fully meet our clients’ needs with confidence and reliability across the Kingdom.

Express Transport Services

Our company provides express transport services to ensure your trucks arrive on time. We work with high efficiency to meet your urgent needs professionally. Our team is ready 24/7 to provide flexible and fast solutions. We guarantee speedy execution while maintaining the highest safety and quality standards.

Heavy Cargo Transport

We have the experience and proper equipment to transport heavy cargo safely and professionally. We ensure precise transport operations regardless of load size. Our team is trained to handle the toughest logistical challenges. We provide reliable heavy transport solutions that efficiently meet the needs of large projects.
